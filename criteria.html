Blog Assessment Criteria

General Information (15%)

Include project title, targeted language competencies, project aim, beneficiaries, and content overview.

Pedagogical & Technological Contents (15%)

Discuss mentoring methods, learning strategies, and technological tools used during the project.

Project Plan (20%)

Present your project planning, step-by-step strategies, and implementation processes.

Project Development (20%)

Feature the project development process, challenges, teamwork, and milestones.

Aesthetics (15%)

Ensure your blog is user-friendly, visually appealing, and provides an enjoyable reading experience.

Reflections (15%)

Share lessons learned, realizations, and suggestions for future project improvement.
